  2. Click the file ending in .exe to initiate the download.
  3. Locate the downloaded file in your browser's download folder or your desktop.

⚙️ Using the Software

Once you complete the installation, the software runs in the background to monitor your computer. You can open the interface from the system tray located near your clock at the bottom right of the screen.

The main menu displays your current protection status. You can perform a full computer scan by clicking the Scan button. This process identifies potential risks and removes threats from your system files.
